Social Media · Reddit

Subreddit research and Reddit strategy for a sports statistics brand

A company behind a sports statistics and analytics platform wants to reach Reddit people interested in players' stats. The goal was to identify Reddit groups that might contain such people and provide marketing tactics. We conducted subreddit research and prepared a Reddit marketing strategy.
Our process: search subreddits by keywords, pick the most relevant groups according to our criteria, extract user activity stats and moderation rules for each, and do a post-by-post analysis of shortlisted subreddits. Delivery is a spreadsheet with subreddits' stats, a strategy document, and a series of table posts.

Regional market research · Europe

Trucking industry in Europe

The company that develops truck accessories wanted to find out the frequent truck driver's challenges. The project included an overview of the truck transportation industry landscape and a study of a truck driver's experience. The state of road freight transportation in Europe was reviewed. The drivers' pain points and challenges were discovered from social media and industry reports.

Regional market research · Europe

Supply chain management business opportunities in Europe

The goal of this project was to identify supply management challenges and associated business opportunities. First, we made a review of Europe's trade routes by the category of products and volume. Then, mapped the product category to the goods types (perishable, hazardous, etc.). From the mapping, we picked the supply chain operation type that addresses the transportation needs of the most demanded products.
